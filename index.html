Sales-Driven Logistics Strategy

Transforming logistics into a sales growth engine through client portfolio expansion and operational discipline.

Business Reality

The company operates three retail stores with strong product diversity. However, sales are heavily dependent on individual customers, creating volatility and limiting scalable growth.

The Core Challenge

Retail Dependence

Small transactions, seasonal demand, limited forecasting.

Logistics Misalignment

Same logistics model used for retail and bulk selling.

Untapped B2B Market

No structured approach to institutions and supermarkets.

Strategic Shift

Growth will not come from selling more products, but from selling differently: moving toward a hybrid sales model combining retail and institutional clients.
